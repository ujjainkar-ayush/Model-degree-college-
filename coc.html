Rules and Regulations to follow during academics

Code of conduct and Important istructions for students.

  1. Students should not violate the vigilance of proffessors regarding regular attendance of all subjects.
  2. Students should not chat or talk in the college premises outside the classroom while classes are going on.
  3. In case of living the college imidiatelly after admission for any reason the amount paid along with the admission application form will not be refunded.
  4. Any kind of porgrams, demostrations, meetings etc. in the college premises should not be taken without the permission of the Honorary Director. Also if any political organization should not be established in the college, otherwise strict action will be taken against the concern.
  5. After taking admission in the college, the rules and regulations of the college will be binding on the student.
  6. College property should not be defaced in any way, damages wil be compensated student will not be allowed to move around the college premises. Also you should have to attend all the programs held by the college from time to time.
  7. The behavior of the student should be neat and disciplined. The Honorary Director shall have the final right to remove the name of any student from the college for indiscipline and other reasons.
  8. Students should keep eye on instructions posted on notice board from time to time and they should need to obey that instructions.
  9. Decision on all disputed questions shall be final and binding on all.

Important Instructions for Guardians and Parents

  1. Parents must read the rules pertaining to attendance, fees and other charges to ensure their wards adhere to this strictly.
  2. It is imperitive for the students to appear for and pass college examinations otherwise he/she will not allowed to appear in the University/Board exams.
  3. Parents must take note of instructions issued to their wards from time to time by the college and must also closely monitor their wards attendance and overall progress.
  4. In the events of students involvement in any,dipute or untoward incident, the concerned parents will have to depose before the pricipal.
  5. The parents are strictly informed that ragging is strictly prohibited. Any student found guilty will br proceeded against under Maharashtra Government Ordinance No. 33 dated 15 May, 1999 and will be expelled from the college forthwith.

Rules for Cancellation of admission & refund of tuition fees.

  1. If the Cancellation is made within 10 days from the admission 75% refund of fees.
  2. If the Cancellation is made between 11-20 days from the admission 50% refund of fees.
  3. If the Cancellation is made after 20 days from admission refund fees is not permitted.
